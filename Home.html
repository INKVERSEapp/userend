INKVERSE

About Us

INKVERSE is an app that connects tattoo artists with potential clients, simplifying finding the right artist, scheduling consultations, and booking tattoo appointments.
It serves as a marketplace and a community hub, catering to seasoned tattoo enthusiasts and first-timers looking for a personalized experience.


Our Vision

To revolutionize the tattoo industry by providing a seamless and inclusive platform that connects individuals with their ideal tattoo artists. INKVERSE will empower users to discover unique artists, book appointments effortlessly, and engage with a vibrant tattoo community.
By fostering a personalized and supportive environment, we aim to make the tattoo experience accessible and enjoyable for everyone.


Our Certificate

INKVERSE prioritize your safety and well-being. To ensure the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness, our team conducts regular inspections and audits to ensure that these studios adhere to the highest standards of hygiene and sanitation.
By using our app, you can easily verify the certification status of any studio before booking your appointment, guaranteeing a safe and enjoyable tattoo experience.
