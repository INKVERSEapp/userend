To do before Tattoo

Step1: Consideration
Make sure the design resonates with you.
Explore different tattoo styles to find one that suits your vision.
Think about where on your body you want the tattoo.

Step2: Find Artist
Look at portfolios to ensure their style aligns with your idea.
Research the shop’s hygiene standards and read reviews.

Step3: Consultation
Schedule a consultation and ask for feedback or suggestions.
Get a quote for the tattoo, including any touch-up policies.

Step4: Prepare Your Skin
Drink plenty of water leading up to the session for healthy skin.
Do not expose the area to the sun or tanning prior to getting tattooed.

Step5: Day before Session
Being well-rested helps you tolerate the pain better.
Have a healthy meal before your appointment to avoid feeling light-headed.
Dress appropriately for the area making it easy for the artist to access the spot.
Avoid Alcohol/Caffeine to make you less sensitive to pain.

Step6: On the session day
Bring a snack and water especially if it’s a long session.
Download a podcast, music, or audiobook to pass the time.
Have aftercare ointments or lotions ready for use.
